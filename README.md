### Q: I'm scared I won't learn Python fast enough
**A:** You learn by building, not studying. The 3 projects will teach you more than 10 courses. Start now, don't wait.

### Q: Should I wait until I'm "expert" before applying?
**A:** No! Apply while learning. Say "entry-level in Python, expert in security." You'll get interviews. They want your domain knowledge.

### Q: What if I don't have real security data?
**A:** Use the sample data in projects. Or download from Kaggle. Or synthesize realistic examples. The algorithm matters more than the data.

### Q: How much time per day do I need?
**A:** 2-3 hours/day for 30 days = 60-90 hours total = job-ready. Less than full-time job, doable alongside work.

### Q: What if I get rejected?
**A:** You will. Everyone does. Ask for feedback. Learn. Apply to next. After 10+ rejections, you'll get offers. It's a numbers game.

### Q: Can I modify the portfolio website?
**A:** Yes! It's HTML. You can customize colors, add projects, change text. Make it yours.

### Q: Should I build these exact projects?
**A:** Build similar projects solving similar problems. The idea matters more than exact replication. Adapt to YOUR interests.

### Q: What if I don't have a GitHub account?
**A:** Create one: github.com/signup (free, 2 minutes). Upload your projects. It's essential for showing code.

### Q: Is 12 years really that valuable?
**A:** YES. Most junior analysts have 0 years domain knowledge. You have 12. That's 12x more. Own it.
